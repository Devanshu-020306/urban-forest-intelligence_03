## ğŸŒ¿ Detectable Diseases

The AI can detect:

1. **Leaf Spot**
   - Brown circular spots on leaves
   - Yellow halos around spots
   - Severity: Medium

2. **Powdery Mildew**
   - White powdery coating
   - Distorted growth
   - Severity: Medium

3. **Root Rot**
   - Wilting despite watering
   - Yellowing from bottom up
   - Severity: High

4. **Bacterial Blight**
   - Water-soaked spots
   - Rapid spread
   - Severity: High

5. **Nutrient Deficiency**
   - Yellowing leaves
   - Stunted growth
   - Severity: Low

6. **Healthy Plants**
   - No disease detected
   - Maintenance tips provided
